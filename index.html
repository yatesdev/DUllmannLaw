Appellate Practice

Ms. Ullmann represents clients in both of Maryland’s appellate courts, the Court of Special Appeals and the Court of Appeals. She has noted appeals with the Circuit Courts, written and filed briefs, drafted Petitions for Writ of Certiorari, and participated in oral arguments. Her appellate experience spans practice areas including family law, juvenile law, and administrative law. She is an approved appellate attorney with the Office of the Public Defender. Ms. Ullmann has numerous unreported opinions from the Court of Special Appeals and one reported opinion.

Criminal Law

Ms. Ullmann represents clients in District Court criminal matters and is a panel attorney with the Office of the Public Defender.

Criminal Cases: theft, controlled dangerous substance, assaults, peace and protective orders, and violations of probation.

Traffic Cases: DUI/DWI, driving while suspended/revoked, speeding/reckless driving matters. Ms. Ullmann can also assist clients with criminal record expungements.
